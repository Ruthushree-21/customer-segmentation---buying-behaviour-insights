# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Buying Behavior Insights Dashboard (Tableau)

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and spending patterns to segment customers into meaningful groups for business decision-making and targeted marketing strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To identify customer segments such as High-Value Customers, Frequent Buyers, and At-Risk Customers using data visualization and analytics in Tableau.

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Customer segmentation based on purchase behavior
- Identification of high-value and at-risk customers
- Analysis of spending patterns and product preferences
- Trend analysis of customer purchases over time
- Interactive dashboards with filters and KPIs

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Tableau
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence

---

## 📈 Dashboards Included
- KPI Overview Dashboard
- Customer Segmentation Analysis
- Spending Behavior Analysis
- Product Category Preference
- Purchase Trend Analysis
